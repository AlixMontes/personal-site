About

Strategy is storytelling with a purpose.

I'm a marketing strategist with a decade of agency experience, now leading brand strategy at Barry's. My work spans integrated campaigns, brand positioning, and the art of making complex problems feel solvable.

Before Barry's, I spent years at agencies including Alma DDB, Creative Theory, and Wunderman Thompson — managing campaigns for Google, Pfizer, Molson Coors, and more. I've earned recognition at Cannes Lions, Clio Health, and the MM+M Awards.

I also teach yoga, speak three languages (English, French, Spanish), and publish a newsletter that applies creative brief methodology to problems beyond advertising.
